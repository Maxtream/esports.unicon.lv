Cup format and main rules:

  • 5 vs 5
  • No team limit
  • One player can play for a single team
  • Format: Playoff brackets
  • Single Elimination
  • Online qualification, LAN finals
  • No rank limit
  • BO3 until Finals
  • BO5 on LAN Finals
  • Only Active Duty maps are allowed, maps can not be the same in one Best of matches.
  • eBot manager will be used for the matches on the server
  • Players must record their game using "record" command in console
  • Qualification games will be streamed in random on the official Twitch channel

Rules:

  • By entering UniCon eSports cup 2016 you agree that you've read and accepted the rules
  • We reserve the right to change, modify, or adapt all rules as deemed appropriate by UniCon eSports
  • All disputing moments and moments that are not mentioned in rules are resolved by UniCon eSports responsible people. Resolution is final and cannot be escalated.
  • Players with offensive nicknames or avatars are not allowed to participate in the cup

Schedule:

  • Registration is open from 13.06.2016 till 26.06.2016.
  • Online qualification goes from 02.07.2016 till 24.07.2016
  • LAN Final is on 06.08.2016 during UniCon 2016
  • By participation in the tournament you agree to travel to the Final (in case you've made it that far) on your own expense. PC and gaming gadgets will be provided by LUDUS Pub. It is allowed to use your own PC and gaming gadgets.

Registration:

  • Registration is done on Battlefy page
  • Team can register using Guest feature
  • Team captain should join our discord channel in order to have communication with admins
  • Substitutes are allowed and can be swapped between BO matches.
  • In order to continue to participate in the cup till the end, core members (3 people with same nicknames and steam profiles), that were initially registered must still be in the team

Preparation:

  • After match announcement in the bracket, teams must discuss in Battlefy chat the best time for both team to play
  • In case teams didn't use Battlefy chat to discuss time, screenshot must be made, where it is seen at what time both parties agreed to play
  • Match must be played in next 3 days after match announcement in the bracket
  • If team can not come to agreement, UniCon eSports admins must be involved to solve the problem
  • In some cases UniCon eSports recording/streaming team will be invited to a match, in this case all 3 parties must agree on time
  • If one of playing parties won't show up on match time or will have insuficient amount of players, team will be disqualified

Map draft:

  • Valve map drafting system will be used. It works the following way:
  • Team in the upper bracket begins the veto process.
  • Team A will ban a map
  • Team B will ban a map
  • Team A will pick a map (played first in the best-of-three)
  • Team B will pick a map (played second in the best of three)
  • Team A will ban a map (in case of BO5 played as third map)
  • Team B will ban a map (in case of BO5 played as fourth map)
  • The remaining map will be used as the 3rd/5th map if the series goes three/five games.

Scoring:

  • Game is played on one map in two half-times of 15 in-game rounds. For each half-time, teams play alternatively terrorist and counter-terrorist, and score one point by winning one in-game round. If one team scores 16 in-game rounds the match ends and the remaining rounds are not to be played. The winner of a match is the team that has won two games. If both teams are tied at the end of a game, MR3 overtimes with 10.000 startmoney will be played until a winner is declared. Teams do not switch sides.

Results:

  • The winning team's captain has to enter the result on the Battlefy match page.
  • In the event of disputes, teams are highly advised to take screenshots of key moments within a map (checkpoints after capture, end of game score screen). If there is a dispute over time and neither team can provide proof, the match may be subject to replay or forfeit at the admin's discretion.

Ingame problems and disqualifications:

  • VAC-banned players are not allowed in the tournament. If a player gets VAC-banned during the tournament it will result in disqualification of the team.
  • First usage of known exploits or bugs will result in a forfeit of cup.
  • A formal complaint regarding behaviour and/or bugs needs to occur no more than 15 minutes after the game ended, otherwise the matter will be considered as closed
  • Admins will reserve the right to disqualify a team based on their behaviour at their sole discretion if something noteworthy is brought to their attention

Spectating and streaming:

  • All spectators and non-affiliated streams are prohibited (that means subs AND coaches/managers)
  • Any sanctioned spectators such as tournament admins and/or affiliated streamers may not be rejected by the teams
  • Players are NOT allowed to live stream their games in this tournament, however players can local record their games for their own use

Prize pool:

  • Prize distribution goes as following:
  • III place – Entrance tickets to UniCon 2016
  • II place – 300€
  • I place – 700€
  • Prize money will be transferred to the teams capitan within one month after the LAN Final.
  • Prize pool may change through the duration of the Cup.

Additional:

  • It is not mandatory to record a demo if you are playing on servers provided by UniCon eSports
