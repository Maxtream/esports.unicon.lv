UniCon eSports Hearthstone November Fireside Gathering is a for free tournament for fans of Blizzard game. Who would love to sit in a warm place from cold weather outside and have a small competition.
So in order to support friendly spirit of competitive, rules are cut-down to simple stuff use common sence as if you would be around your friends.

Critical rules:

  • LAN event
  • Region: Europe
  • 32 players limit
  • BYOD (Bring Your Own Device)
  • No rank limit
  • Double Elimination bracket
  • Format: 4 picks, 1 ban, Conquest (BO5), until all 3 decks won.
  • Match format: Conquest
  • Game Mode: Standard
  • Tournament is FREE

Just rules

  • By registering and participating in UniCon eSports event you agree that you've read and accepted the rules
  • We reserve the right to change, modify, or adapt all rules as deemed appropriate by UniCon eSports at all time
  • All disputing moments and moments that are not mentioned in rules are resolved by UniCon eSports responsible people.

Registration

  • Registration is happening "on set" at UniCon Cafe before 14:30. Cafe opens at 14:00
  • To register you need to come to the desk, tell your battle tag correctly and 4 of your classes.

Preparation

  • Before the match starts you must ask your opponent play what they play and ban one of the class
  • When match is announced, you must sit beside your opponent and challenge each other through local feature or by adding each other on Battle.Net friend list and start the match.

Results

  • Results must be told to Admins after the matches are over
