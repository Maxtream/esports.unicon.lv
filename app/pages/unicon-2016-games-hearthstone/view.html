Cup format and main rules:

  • Region: Europe
  • 64 players limit
  • BYOD / 12 tablets will be provided by our partners SAMSUNG Latvija.
  • No rank limit
  • LAN qualifications and finals
  • Single Elimination
  • Format: 4 picks, 1 ban, Conquest (BO5), until all 3 decks won.
  • Valid UniCon 2016 ticket is needed for participation.

Rules:

  • By entering UniCon eSports cup you agree that you've read and accepted the rules
  • We reserve the right to change, modify, or adapt all rules as deemed appropriate by UniCon eSports
  • All disputing moments and moments that are not mentioned in rules are resolved by UniCon eSports responsible people. Resolution is final and cannot be escalated.

Registration:

  • Valid UniCon 2016 ticket is needed for participation.
  • Registration will be closed on 6th of August at 14:00 Latvia, Riga time (EEST)
  • Registration is done on Battlefy page
  • Registeration can be done using Guest feature
  • In order to register and/or check in, please find a booth on UniCon Latvia 2016 and tell admins your Battle Tag

Preparation:

  • Games starts at 15:00 on 6th of August.
  • All games must be played almost simultaneously if not stated otherwise by UniCon eSports Admins
  • You must have 4 heroes/decks ready to play without duplicate hero available in your deck list.
  • Before the match starts you must ask admins for classes that your opponent play, ban one of them, you can ask to do it silently without opponent knowing what you will ban, Admin will notify you both afterwards
  • If opponent didn't show up in next 15 minutes after the match was announced if not stated otherwise by Admins, opponent is disqualified.
  • When match is announced, you must sit beside your opponent and display your deck list, after that you must add each other to Battle.Net friend list and start the match.
  • All matches must be played without leaving the match lobby.
  • If game crashed and you're not allowed to get back to the game in time, please show your game screen to your opponent and call for Admin immidiately to record that. After that try to continue the match where you left it. Opponent is allowed to make his move, but without taking advantage of timeout, he must wait for a "rope" to appear.

Scoring:

  • Match format: Conquest
  • Player must win one game with each of his three decks to win the match.
  • When a player wins a game, the deck used by the winning player cannot be used afterwards till the end of BO5 match.
  • The losing player can keep the same deck used or switch to a different one of their choice (the one that didn't won before)

Results:

  • Results must be told to Admins immidiately after the matches are over
  • We do advice you to take screenshots of Win Screen of the game, in case of dispute moments. Please find out before the Cup how to make screenshot on your device or device that will be provided to you

Game settings:

  • Game Mode: Standart (No Naxxramas or GvG)
  • After entering lobby it is not allowed to leave without penalty

Spectating and streaming:

  • All specators must be forbidden, including friends.
  • When semi final games starts spectating should be enabled again for UniCon eSports streaming team to be able to stream the games

Prize pool:

  • Prize distribution goes as following:
  • III place – 50€
  • II place – 150€
  • I place – 300€ and a trip to IEM Katowice 2017
  • Prize money will be transferred within one month after the LAN Final.
  • Prize pool may change through the duration of the Cup.
