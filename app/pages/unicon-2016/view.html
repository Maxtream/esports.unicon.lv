UniCon eSports is a division of "UniCon Latvia" which main objective is the development of esports in Latvia and the region in general. For this purpose we are organising high quality Online and LAN esports tournaments and events. Our first major project is "UniCon eSports Cup 2016" which will have three disciplines (Overwatch, CS:GO and Hearthstone), and will have online qualifications and LAN finals, which will take place at UniCon 2016.
